THE DESIGNER

The founder of our bridal salon is the incredibly talented and dedicated designer, Emma Stone. Emma has always dreamed of creating something unique and elegant that would captivate the hearts of our clients. Thanks to her passion for beauty and impeccable taste, she has gone from being a design student to owning one of the most prestigious bridal salons in the city.

Emma has years of experience in the fields of design and fashion. She obtained her higher education at leading art universities, where she studied not only the technical aspects of design but also philosophy and aesthetics. Working with top brands and renowned designers, Emma gained valuable experience, which she now successfully embodies in every detail of our salon.

Her impeccable taste, creative approach, and unique ability to sense our clients' desires allow us to create captivating looks for the wedding day. Emma Stone is not just the founder but also the heart of our salon, who with great love and care helps every bride find the perfect look for the most important day of her life.
